Consilium

Consilium, an app I designed for a 24-hour designathon held at IIIT-D in New Delhi, India. It is an app which aims to allows students across the world to get specifics of college applications, resume/portfolio, career counselling, aptitude test etc.

After a gruel and tiring challenge, our team emerged as the Overall Winners and the best in UX for the competition. I felt proud as it was my first win as the solo designer on the team, and earning the judges' appreciation for the UX topped it all.
