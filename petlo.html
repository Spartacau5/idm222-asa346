Petlo

Petlo, a pet adoption app that myself and our team of 4 designed for a 24-hour designathon held at IIIT-D in New Delhi, India. It is an app which aims to make the process of pet adoption easier. Our team emerged in the Top 3 for the competition. This was my first competition designing User Interface, and I was satisfied with the result and how much I learnt in just a day.
